Barack Obama has told CNN's Wolf Blitzer, that he will NOT vacate the Oval office if Donald J. Trump is elected as the 45th President of the United States.He claims he is "fully prepared" to ignore the popular vote if it means stopping Trump. Having found what he believes is a little known loophole that would allow him to remain in charge until a re-election is called.
