Trump cancels event in Phoenix, then tweets confirming event Arizona News Trump cancels event in Arizona for Wednesday Presidential candidate Donald Trump canceled his event for Wednesday that was supposed to take place in Phoenix at the Hyatt Regency but then tweeted that he will be in Phoenix after all.

Some confusion on Friday involving Donald Trump's visit to the Valley next Wednesday.

A few days ago his campaign announced he would be coming to Phoenix for a GOP unity rally held at the Hyatt Regency in Phoenix on Wednesday.

But on Friday afternoon his campaign said the event was cancelled.

A couple hours later, Trump himself said he will be in Phoenix on Wednesday.

Trump was scheduled to give a speech on policy at the event .

Will be in Phoenix, Arizona on Wednesday. Changing venue to much larger one. Demand is unreal. Polls looking great! #ImWithYou -- Donald J. Trump (@realDonaldTrump) August 26, 2016
